Scaler evolved from InterviewBit, a technical interview prep platform, into a more comprehensive online "tech-versity" or career accelerator with high-touch elements like 1:1 mentorship, live cohort-based classes, community, and gamification. ### Background and What He's Built

Scaler has expanded into programs like Scaler School of Technology (a 4-year undergrad-focused residential/offline program in partnership models) and even business/tech leadership angles (e.g., Scaler School of Business).

He is based in/associated with San Francisco but deeply focused on the Indian tech talent pipeline, with a vision to bridge the gap between Indian engineering education and global (especially Silicon Valley) standards. Scaler emphasizes practical skills, career outcomes, and preparing for real-world impact over rote learning or degrees alone.
