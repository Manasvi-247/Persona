how it works

No magic. Just good prompts.

Each mentor has a hand-crafted system prompt — researched, annotated, with few-shot examples and chain-of-thought instructions. The LLM isn't pretending; it's reasoning in their voice.

01

Pick a mentor

Three personas, each with their own system prompt, tone, suggestion chips, and bio panel. Switch any time — the conversation resets cleanly.

02

Ask anything real

Career bets, DSA pain, system design, tough decisions. The prompts include constraints — these mentors won't sugarcoat or hand-hold.

03

Get a real answer

Chain-of-thought reasoning happens before the reply. Each response sounds like the actual person, ends with a question, and stays in character.
